EDB BigAnimal is a fully managed database-as-a-service that runs in your cloud account or BigAnimal's cloud account, where it's operated by the Postgres experts. BigAnimal makes it easy to set up, manage, and scale your databases. You can use [PostgreSQL](https://www.enterprisedb.com/docs/supported-open-source/postgresql/), [EDB Postgres Advanced Server](/epas/latest/) with Oracle compatibility, or [EDB Postgres Extended Server](/pge/latest/) with extreme high availability for your Postgres database. 

To sample EDB BigAnimal, [get started with $300 in free credits](/biganimal/latest/free_trial/).
