**Gen AI Builder** is the developer-facing environment of **AI Factory**, designed to help you build advanced agentic AI applications on trusted data and governed infrastructure.

Builder gives you fine-grained control over **Knowledge Bases**, **Retrievers**, **Rulesets**, **Structures**, and **Tools** — letting you compose AI-powered applications that integrate deeply with your business context.

**Gen AI Builder is the foundation for building Sovereign AI agents and data-powered AI workflows in AI Factory.**

* * *

## Core Capabilities

-   Manage **Knowledge Bases** and power RAG-based experiences
-   Create and manage **Retrievers** for optimized semantic and structured search
-   Define **Rulesets** to control assistant tone, behavior, and compliance
-   Build and deploy **Tools** to extend agent capabilities with API calls, calculations, and more
-   Package and run **Structures** — complex Griptape-powered workflows and data pipelines
-   Monitor and analyze **Threads** — conversation history and system behavior

## When to use Gen AI Builder

Use Gen AI Builder when:

-   You need **fine-grained control** of what your assistants retrieve and how they behave.
-   You want to integrate **structured and unstructured data** into your agents.
-   You need to build **trusted, compliant, production-grade agents** — not simple chatbot demos.
-   You want to **extend agents with Tools** that connect to your real-world systems.
-   You are building **multi-step AI pipelines and workflows** using Structures.

* * *

**AI Factory Gen AI Builder** gives you the tools to build advanced, extensible, enterprise-ready AI applications — entirely under your governance and with full visibility.
