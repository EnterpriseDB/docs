EDB Postgres Distributed (PGD) provides a suite of tools and methodologies for maintaining the health and evolution of your multi-master clusters. Managing lifecycle operations effectively ensures that your high-availability environment remains performant and secure through software updates, migrations, and configuration tuning.

### Section Overview

* [Deploying and configuring](deploying) Instructions for provisioning PGD clusters across different environments, including manual installations, TPA-automated deployments, CloudNativePG deployments, and Hybrid Manager integrations.

* [Upgrading PGD](upgrades) Detailed workflows for performing minor and major version upgrades for both the underlying PostgreSQL engine and the PGD extension with minimal downtime.

* [Testing and tuning for performance](testing-tuning) Guidance on benchmarking your distributed workloads using `pgd-bench` and optimizing system parameters for maximum throughput.

* [Performing backup and recovery](backup-restore) Best practices for securing your data, including physical and logical backup strategies tailored for distributed multi-master topologies.
