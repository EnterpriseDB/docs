## Upgrading to EDB Postgres Distributed 6

You can't upgrade to EDB Postgres Distributed 6.0.0 from EDB Postgres Distributed 5.x or earlier. Instead, [upgrade to EDB Postgres Distributed 6.1.0](/pgd/6.1/upgrades/) or later.
