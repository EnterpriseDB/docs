Setting up an EDB Transporter migration consists of a number of steps.

1.  [Create a target database cluster](create_database) in the EDB PostgresÂ® AI Console.
1.  [Prepare the schema](prepare_schema) with Migration Portal and migrate the schema to the target database.
1.  [Prepare your source Oracle or Postgres database](preparing_db) with `sqlplus` or `psql`.
1.  [Install EDB Transporter](installing) on your machine with your terminal.
1.  [Configure the EDB Transporter source reader](config_reader) on your machine with your terminal.
1.  [Create a new migration](create_migration) in the EDB Postgres AI Console.
1.  [Mark the Migration as completed](mark_completed) in the EDB Postgres AI Console.
1.  [Apply constraints](apply_constraints) to the new database.
1.  [Verify the migration completed successfully](verify_migration) with LiveCompare.
1.  [Remove customer software](remove_software).
