When you upgrade your WarehousePG cluster, you must perform some additional steps in order to bring your PXF configuration up to date.

*WarehousePG 6.x to 6.newer upgrade*: You may be required to perform PXF upgrade actions when you [upgrade from a previous WarehousePG 6.x version](upgrade_pxf_6x.mdx).
