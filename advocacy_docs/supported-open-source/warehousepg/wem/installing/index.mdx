!!! Note
This guide assumes you have installed Prometheus and Loki. 
!!!

The installation process consists of the following steps:

1. Verify the [Prerequisites](prerequisites).
1. [Install the WHPG Extension](collector) on your WarehousePG (WHPG) cluster.
1. [Install and configure WEM](wem) to visualize your WHPG metrics.
