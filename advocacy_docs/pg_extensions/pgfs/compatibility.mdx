## Supported platforms

- Ubuntu 22.04 LTS and 24.04 LTS on x86/64
- Debian 12 (Bookworm) on x86/64 and ARM64
- Red Hat / RHEL 9 and 8 on x86/64
- Red Hat / RHEL 9 on ARM64

## Not currently supported

- SLES
- Debian versions earlier than 12
- Non-Linux platforms

## Supported PostgreSQL versions

- EDB Postgres Advanced Server 14, 15, 16, and 17
- EDB Postgres Extended 14, 15, 16, and 17
- PostgreSQL 14, 15, 16, and 17
