EDB Postgres AI Databases are available for on-premises deployment. 

_The on-premises deployment of EDB Postgres AI Database are a self-managed deployment, which can use the EDB Postgres AI Estate and Agent components to manage and integrate the on-premises deployment alongside the EDB Postgres AI Database Cloud Service through the EDB Postgres AI Console's single pane of glass._
