To pull operator or operand images from EDB container registries, you need to: 

1. [Get your token](../get_your_token).
1. [Choose your registry](choose_your_registry).
1. [Deploy operators](deploy_operators).

!!! SeeAlso Next steps
    
    Once you've designed your system and are preparing to go to production, you'll want to configure a local registry
    to provide access to EDB images, custom images, and system or third-party images. This is always a good idea for
    performance and reliability, but if your requirements include air-gapped installation or sovereignty-compliance, 
    then a local, private registry is an essential part of the system.
