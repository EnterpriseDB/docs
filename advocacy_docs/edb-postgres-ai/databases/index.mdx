## Postgres options 

Building on decades of Postgres expertise, the EDB PostgresÂ® AI databases are the core of the EDB Postgres AI platform. 

-   [EDB Postgres Advanced Server](/epas/latest) can take on Oracle workloads.
-   [EDB Postgres Extended Server](/pge/latest) designed for large-scale, mission-critical enterprise workloads.

- [PostgreSQL](/supported-open-source/postgresql) is the world's most advanced open source database, and the foundation for all EDB Postgres databases.

And for distributed high-availability and fault tolerance for mission-critical workloads, you can use EDB Postgres Advanced Server and EDB Postgres Extended server in combination with EDB Postgres Distributed: 

-   [EDB Postgres Distributed](/pgd/latest)
