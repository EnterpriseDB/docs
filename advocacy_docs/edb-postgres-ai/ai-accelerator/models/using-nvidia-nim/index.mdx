---
title: Using Nvidia NIM models
---

You can use Nvidia NIM models with EDB Postgres AI Accelerator. The models can be running in the Nvidia cloud or running within your environment, under your control.

This guide shows you how to use Nvidia NIM models with EDB Postgres AI Accelerator in both scenarios.

* [In the Nvidia cloud](using-nim-in-nvidia-cloud)
* [In your environment](using-nim-in-your-environment)
