**AI Factory** provides a flexible platform for building **trusted, scalable AI-powered applications** across industries — combining your enterprise data, AI pipelines, model serving, and conversational agents.

This page highlights **common solution patterns** and **examples by industry** — along with links to relevant [How-To Guides](./how-to/gen-ai) and [Learning Paths](./paths) to help you build them.

For more technical patterns, see [Use Cases](./use-cases).

---

## Financial Services

AI Factory helps financial services organizations deliver **secure, compliant AI** for customer experience and internal operations.

### Example goals:

- Improve customer service with **automated agents** grounded in proprietary knowledge.
- Enable secure, explainable **internal knowledge search** for advisors and staff.
- Automate document-heavy workflows (KYC, onboarding).

### Example solutions:

- **Client Support Chatbots** powered by [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant), backed by [Knowledge Bases](./how-to/gen-ai/create-knowledge-base) and governed by [Rulesets](./how-to/gen-ai/create-ruleset).
- **Internal Knowledge Search** combining [Retrievers](./how-to/gen-ai/create-retriever) and [Knowledge Bases](./how-to/gen-ai/create-knowledge-base), optionally enriched via [Pipelines](../pipeline/gettingstarted).
- **Automated Document Processing** with [Pipelines](../pipeline/gettingstarted), using [OCR](../pipeline/preparers/examples/perform_ocr), [Summarization](../pipeline/preparers/examples/summarize_text), and AI-driven extraction.

## Healthcare and Life Sciences

AI Factory supports **regulated, compliant AI** for clinical and operational use cases.

### Example goals:

- Improve clinical decision support with **literature-aware agents**.
- Automate **summarization** of complex medical documents.
- Deliver personalized **patient-facing assistants**.

### Example solutions:

- **Medical Literature Search** using [Knowledge Bases](./how-to/gen-ai/create-knowledge-base), optimized [Retrievers](./how-to/gen-ai/create-retriever), and conversational [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant).
- **Clinical Document Summarization** with document prep via [Pipelines](../pipeline/gettingstarted) and high-performance inference using [Model Serving](./how-to/model-serving).
- **Patient Support Assistants** built with [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant), compliant [Rulesets](./how-to/gen-ai/create-ruleset), and external integrations via [Tools](./how-to/gen-ai/create-tool).

## Retail and E-Commerce

AI Factory enables **personalized, AI-powered experiences** for retail and e-commerce applications.

### Example goals:

- Deliver **semantic product search** experiences.
- Power **personalized recommendations** across channels.
- Automate **customer support workflows**.

### Example solutions:

- **Semantic Product Search** with embeddings served via [Model Serving](./how-to/model-serving), queried using tuned [Retrievers](./how-to/gen-ai/create-retriever).
- **Personalized Recommendations** combining [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant), [Retrievers](./how-to/gen-ai/create-retriever), and **real-time API integrations** with [Tools](./how-to/gen-ai/create-tool).
- **Customer Service Automation** with conversational [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant), [Knowledge Bases](./how-to/gen-ai/create-knowledge-base), and brand-safe [Rulesets](./how-to/gen-ai/create-ruleset).

## Manufacturing and Industrial

AI Factory brings **AI-driven knowledge and automation** to the shop floor and field operations.

### Example goals:

- Build **maintenance assistants** that surface engineering knowledge.
- Enable **search across technical manuals and SOPs**.
- Automate **document understanding** and reporting.

### Example solutions:

- **Knowledge-driven Maintenance Assistants** built with [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant), connected to [Knowledge Bases](./how-to/gen-ai/create-knowledge-base), and powered by API-based [Tools](./how-to/gen-ai/create-tool).
- **Document Search across Manuals and SOPs** using [Pipelines](../pipeline/gettingstarted) for document processing, and RAG with tuned [Retrievers](./how-to/gen-ai/create-retriever).

## Public Sector and Government

AI Factory helps governments and public sector organizations deliver **transparent, auditable AI** in sensitive contexts.

### Example goals:

- Support **internal policy search and compliance checks**.
- Power **citizen-facing service portals**.
- Enable **knowledge-grounded AI experiences** with governance.

### Example solutions:

- **Internal Policy Search and Compliance QA** with trusted [Knowledge Bases](./how-to/gen-ai/create-knowledge-base), [Retrievers](./how-to/gen-ai/create-retriever), and auditable [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant).
- **Citizen Support Portals** built with conversational [Assistants](./how-to/gen-ai/create-assistant), external system integrations via [Tools](./how-to/gen-ai/create-tool), and governed [Rulesets](./how-to/gen-ai/create-ruleset).
