Barman is a tool for managing backup and recovery of PostgreSQL databases. It is designed for business critical databases and provides features such as backup catalogues, incremental backup, retention policies, and remote recovery. 

Barman is integrated with EDB Postgres AI and can be used to manage backups of your EDB Postgres AI databases.
