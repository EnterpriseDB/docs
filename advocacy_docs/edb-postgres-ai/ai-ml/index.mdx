EDB Postgres AI AI/ML.

_EDB Postgres AI AI/ML is a set of tools, utilities and extensions that are designed to help you build and deploy machine learning and artificial intelligence models on your EDB Postgres AI databases. Building machine learning and artificial intelligence models on your EDB Postgres AI databases has never been easier._
