Kubernetes is the operational foundation of the EDB Developer experience. It powers [Hybrid Manager](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/hybrid-manager/), the control plane that unifies Postgres and AI workloads into a single, governable infrastructure. Whether you’re deploying Postgres clusters, scaling AI inference with GPUs, or managing pipelines for document intelligence, Kubernetes orchestrates it all in a cloud-native, consistent way.

Within this section of the [EDB Developer Portal](/portal/kubernetes), you’ll find practical guidance and strategic context for running Kubernetes with EDB Postgres AI products. Resources are structured by role, by technical function, and by specific platform responsibilities.

* * *

## Kubernetes and Hybrid Manager

[Hybrid Manager](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/hybrid-manager/) relies on Kubernetes to run:

-   [Postgres clusters](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/hybrid-manager/overview/database_types/)
-   [AI Factory Gen AI workloads](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/hybrid-manager/ai-factory/gen-ai/)
-   [Model Serving endpoints](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/hybrid-manager/ai-factory/models)
-   [GPU resource management](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/hybrid-manager/ai-factory/gpu-recommended)
-   [Vector-enabled search](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/ai-factory/vector-engine/)
-   [Data pipelines](/edb-postgres-ai/latest/ai-factory/pipeline/knowledge_base/)
-   [Observability and diagnostics](/portal/kubernetes/learn/how-to/hybrid-manager/use-edb-observability-stack)

These workloads run inside your own Kubernetes clusters—on public cloud or on-prem—enabling sovereign data control and workload portability across environments.

## Role-Based Guidance

Understand how Kubernetes fits your team’s needs and responsibilities:

-   [Cloud Architects](/portal/kubernetes/learn/explained/for/cloud-architects): architecture, topology, and multi-cluster strategies
-   [Platform Engineers](/portal/kubernetes/learn/explained/for/platform-engineers): provisioning, automation, and cluster configuration
-   [DBAs](/portal/kubernetes/learn/explained/for/dbas): persistent storage, backups, scaling, and Postgres resource tuning
-   [Software Engineers](/portal/kubernetes/learn/explained/for/software-engineers): service reliability, workload integration, CI/CD
-   [Site Reliability Engineers (SREs)](/portal/kubernetes/learn/explained/for/sres): operations, observability, and resilience engineering
-   [Security Engineers](/portal/kubernetes/learn/explained/for/security-engineers): RBAC, network policy, secrets management
-   [Operations Support](/portal/kubernetes/learn/explained/for/operations-support): incident triage, cluster health, support runbooks
